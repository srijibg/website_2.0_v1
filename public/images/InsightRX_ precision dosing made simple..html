Cloud-based platform for precision dosing

The Insight RX software platform and API leverages peer-reviewed mathematical models, patient demographics, physiological characteristics, pharmacogenomics, drug concentrations and biomarkers to provide optimized patient-specific dosing and treatment strategies, and clinically relevant analytics to healthcare practitioners and pharmaceutical companies.

The platform is able to reach and maintain desired therapeutic drug levels more quickly and accurately to ensure treatment effectiveness. Standardize and optimize clinical dosing guidelines to reduce adverse drug reactions.

About Us

We love what we do

Sirj Goswami

Holds a doctorate degree from the department of Bioengineering and Therapeutic Sciences at UCSF, with a research focus in genomics, pharmacokinetics and pharmacometrics. Sirj was a Discovery Fellow at UCSF and has over four years of industry experience at Genentech and Pfizer, working in drug development, spanning departments of biostatistics and clinical pharmacology.

His passion is to bring genomics and advanced analytics to the forefront of healthcare.

Ron Keizer

A pharmacist by training with a PhD in pharmacometrics, Ron has over 20 peer-reviewed publications in pharmacometrics, focusing on the application of mathematical-statistical models in drug development or drug treatment. Ron is also a software engineer and created and collaborated on several popular tools in pharmacometrics. He is the creator of Pirana, an interface for applied modeling & simulation that is used by 25+ research groups in pharmaceutical industry and over 300+ academic researchers. After a post-doc at Uppsala University, Ron joined as a senior researcher within the Savic lab at UCSF.

Ranvir Mangat

A bioengineer by training Ranvir has over 5 years of experience as a pharmacometrics researcher in the Clinical Pharmacology Group at Genentech. Ranvir has led PK/PD analytics for a number of drug candidates in clinical development for early and late stage clinical trials, including global regulatory filings and post-marketing Phase IV studies for the blockbuster HER2 targeted drug franchise. His interests are in applying next generation software applications to leverage real world clinical data and facilitate drug development.
